Easily track your read habits! Whether you are an avid reader or trying to make a habit out of reading, Bibliio is for you.

- Add a book to your reading list
- Automatically get an estimated finish date, which can configure based on how much you want to read
- Set reminders to read
- Watch your archive grow with all the books you've finished

Bibliio is open source and is actively being developed, with many improvements and features still to come. If you'd like to see a feature incorporated, or if you'd like to report a bug, you're strongly encouraged to file an issue at https://github.com/atecle/Bibliio/Issues
